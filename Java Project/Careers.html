Career Opportunities

The Indian Army offers a wide range of career opportunities for individuals looking to serve the nation. Some of the key roles include:

  • Soldier General Duty
  • Technical Entry
  • Army Nursing
  • Short Service Commission
  • Permanent Commission

Eligibility Criteria

To join the Indian Army, candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria:

  • Age: 17.5 to 21 years for Soldier posts, and up to 27 years for Officer posts.
  • Education: 10th/12th pass for Soldier posts, Graduation for Officer posts.
  • Physical Standards: Must meet the physical fitness criteria as specified.

Application Process

The application process for joining the Indian Army involves the following steps:

  1. Visit the official Indian Army recruitment website.
  2. Fill out the online application form.
  3. Submit the required documents.
  4. Prepare for the written examination and physical tests.
  5. Attend the interview and medical examination.
