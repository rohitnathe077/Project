Overview

The Indian Army is committed to supporting its veterans through various services and programs designed to assist them in their transition to civilian life and to ensure their well-being.

Veteran Benefits

Veterans are entitled to a range of benefits, including:

  • Pension Schemes: Financial support for retired personnel.
  • Medical Benefits: Access to healthcare services and facilities.
  • Education Assistance: Scholarships and educational programs for veterans and their families.
  • Employment Support: Job placement services and vocational training programs.

Counseling Services

The Indian Army provides counseling services to help veterans cope with various challenges, including:

  • Mental Health Support: Access to psychologists and counselors for mental health issues.
  • Family Counseling: Support for families of veterans to address relationship and adjustment issues.
  • Substance Abuse Programs: Assistance for veterans dealing with addiction and recovery.
