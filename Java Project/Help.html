Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I join the Indian Army?

To join the Indian Army, you can apply through the official recruitment website or attend recruitment rallies held across the country.

2. What are the eligibility criteria for joining?

The eligibility criteria vary based on the position. Generally, candidates must meet age, educational, and physical standards.

3. How can I track my application status?

You can track your application status by visiting the official Indian Army recruitment website and entering your details in the application status section.
