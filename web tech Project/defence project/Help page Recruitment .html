About Recruitment

The Indian Army offers a variety of opportunities for individuals looking to serve the nation. Recruitment is conducted for various positions, including soldiers, officers, and technical roles.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for recruitment in the Indian Army, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • Age: Minimum 17.5 years and maximum 21 years for soldiers; varies for officers.
  • Education: 10th/12th pass for soldiers; graduation for officers.
  • Physical Standards: Candidates must meet specific height, weight, and medical standards.

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for recruitment:

  1. Visit the official Indian Army recruitment website.
  2. Register and create an account.
  3. Fill out the application form with the required details.
  4. Upload necessary documents and photographs.
  5. Submit the application and take a printout for future reference.

Note: Keep an eye on the official website for updates on recruitment rallies and notifications.
