Gallantry Awards

The Gallantry Awards are given for acts of bravery and valor in the face of the enemy. The major gallantry awards include:

  • Param Vir Chakra: The highest military decoration awarded for the display of exceptional courage in the face of the enemy.
  • Mahavir Chakra: The second highest military decoration awarded for acts of bravery in the battlefield.
  • Vir Chakra: Awarded for acts of bravery in the battlefield, it is the third highest military decoration.

Service Awards

Service Awards recognize long and distinguished service in the Indian Army. Some notable service awards include:

  • Ati Vishisht Seva Medal: Awarded for exceptional service or performance of duty.
  • Vishisht Seva Medal: Awarded for distinguished service of a high order.
  • Long Service and Good Conduct Medal: Awarded for long and meritorious service.

Commendation Awards

Commendation Awards are given for commendable service and performance. They include:

  • Chief of Army Staff Commendation: Awarded for commendable service in peace and war.
  • General Officer Commanding Commendation: Awarded for outstanding service in a specific area.
